SSH Heatmap

I used meesaltena' SSHHeatmap script to make an SSH heatmap of failed login attempts. It refreshes every hour. I've moved on to public key authentication and disabled password log in so it's empty right now, but I don't feel like taking it down just yet. You can take a look here.
