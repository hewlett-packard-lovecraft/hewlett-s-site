The vast building loomed in front of him in the sandstorm. It was difficult to judge it’s height, but he was glad to find shelter from the sandstorm. Wanderer’s supplies were running low, and he was lost. He used his staff to pry the ancient doors open, and walked inside.

The theme park’s security system was activated. The clocks recorded fifty years since the last intruder, and half the cameras required replacement. Nevertheless, it dutifully sent a prerecorded message along phone lines that no longer existed to a police station that had not seen use in a generation.
